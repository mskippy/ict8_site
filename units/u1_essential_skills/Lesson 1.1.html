2) Digital Citizenship Agreement

We will review expectations for devices, appropriate AI use, and respectful communication online.

  • Phones in the holder - screen to the wall and camera down.
  • AI tools: allowed for learning (drafting, feedback, idea generation) — always cite and personalize.
  • Kind, inclusive language; ask consent before sharing photos/videos.
  • No food or drink in the lab
  • Respect the space, your peers, yourself

3) Fun Activity — Origami Crane

Practice carefully following online instructions.

  1. Open the provided tutorial and follow along step‑by‑step.
  2. If you get stuck, use the pause/rewind features; compare your fold to the example image.
  3. Hand in your finished crane at the end of class.
  4. If you want, fold another origami object to keep for yourself
