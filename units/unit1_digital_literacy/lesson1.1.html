Lesson 1.1 – What is Digital Literacy?

Learning Intentions

  • Define digital literacy and why it matters in school/life.
  • Identify examples of responsible and irresponsible digital behaviors.

Do Now (3 min)

On a sticky note (or Teams post), write one thing you do online daily and why.

Steps

  1. Skim this overview: What is digital literacy?
  2. With a partner, list 3 skills you think matter most for Grade 8s.
  3. Class share: add your top skill to the board/Teams.
