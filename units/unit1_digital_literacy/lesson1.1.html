Task 2: Search & Fold an Origami Crane

Search the web for instructions and fold a paper crane. Hand your crane in at the front when done.

Search tips (choose good instructions)
  • Try queries like “origami crane easy step by step” or “origami crane beginner video”.
  • Prefer results with clear step images or a short (3–7 min) video.
  • Skim comments/ratings to see if beginners found it helpful.
  • If stuck, try a different result—compare two sources.
